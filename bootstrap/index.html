ASTON MARTIN WINGS SERIES

Aston Martin has a long and proud association with aviation and the Royal Air Force. Its three manufacturing locations are sited on the former RAF Stations at Gaydon, Wellesbourne and St Athan. Active runways continue to operate at Wellesbourne and St Athan and indeed the new DBX production facility in St Athan has been built inside former RAF hangers.

This heritage and strong connection to the RAF has lead Aston Martin to bring its aviation projects together under Aston Martin Wings, an exciting program which will see Q by Aston Martin deliver further limited edition models linked to aviation over the coming years.

About

Aston Martin’s vision is to be the world’s most desirable, ultra-luxury British brand, creating the most exquisitely addictive performance cars. 2020 saw Lawrence Stroll become the company’s Executive Chairman, alongside significant new investment, a move that led to Aston Martin’s return to the pinnacle of motorsport with the Aston Martin Cognizant Formula One™ Team and commenced new era for the iconic British marque.
